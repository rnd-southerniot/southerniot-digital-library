# SOP: site_survey (RAK7266)

## Goal
Confirm mounting, power, grounding, and backhaul readiness for the RAK7266.

## Steps
1. Confirm proposed mount location is physically accessible and secure.
2. Confirm power source (PoE or DC) and cable routing.
3. Confirm grounding point is available and safe to use.
4. Confirm backhaul (`ethernet` or `lte`) and quality for persistent connectivity.
5. Capture 4–5 photos with tags required by the manifest (mount, sky view, power, internet).
6. Run validations in `validations/` and resolve blockers before leaving site.

## Outputs
- CRM comment: site survey summary + risks + photos attached
